The influx characters are 10,000 uniquely generated characters.

See the marketplace instructions below to acquire your very own scientists. You should also join the Discord Chat, which includes a bot that announces all bids, offers and sales in realtime.

 No two are exactly alike, and each one of them can be officially owned by a single person on the Ethereum blockchain. Originally, they could be claimed for free by anybody with an Ethereum wallet, but all 10,000 were quickly claimed. Now they must be purchased from someone via the marketplace that's also embedded in the blockchain. Via this market you can buy, bid on, and offer Scientists for sale. The zoomable image above is connected to the Ethereum network and has been colored to show you the status of every Scientists in the market. Scientists with a blue background have been claimed. Scientists with a red background are available for sale by their owner. Finally, Scientists with a purple background have an active bid on them. You can click to zoom in on a Punk and reveal its details.

How do I get a Scientist?

Responsive block with counters and media.

1

Metamask

Download and install a Chrome browser plugin called MetaMask. This will allow websites (that you authorize) access to your Ethereum account.

2

Buy ETH

If you made a new account, buy some Ether. The MetaMask plugin has a button that will allow you to buy Ether from Coinbase.

3

OpenSea

Once you have the plugin installed, this website will recognize it and add buttons that allow you to bid on, buy and sell punks directly in the interface.
